    • Our Study

    The purpose of our study was to learn how cybersecurity tools are used in social media. We interviewed 10 users who answered a couple of questions regarding how they protect their data online.

    Statistics from our study

    50% of the participants stated that they did not use any tools to protect their accounts online.

    40% of the participants did not know of any tools they could use.

    40% of the participants had their accounts compromised at some point.
